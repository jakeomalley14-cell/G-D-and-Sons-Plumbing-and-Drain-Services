The Owner

A Licensed Journeyman Plumber Who Shows Up Himself

G, D & Sons Plumbing and Drain Services is the work of one person: Glen White, a licensed journeyman plumber based in Chicopee, Massachusetts. Glen has been working in the trade for over 11 years, and he launched his own business professionally in October 2025 to bring that experience directly to homeowners and businesses across Western Mass.

"With over 11 years of hands-on experience, I'm passionate about what I do."

As a family-owned, one-person operation, GD & Sons Plumbing ensures personalized service and attention to detail on every job. There's no dispatcher, no rotating crew, and no handoffs. When you call, you talk to Glen. When he arrives at your home, he's the one doing the work — and the one standing behind it with a 90-day guarantee.

From sewer ejector pumps and toilet rebuilds to drain cleaning and full pipe repairs, Glen handles the full range of residential and small commercial plumbing across Chicopee, Hampden County, Berkshire County, and the surrounding areas of Western Massachusetts.
