Fixture FAQs

Can I supply my own fixture or do I need to buy through you?

You can absolutely buy your own faucet, toilet, or vanity — Glen will install it. Many customers prefer this approach for budget control or specific style preferences.

Do you install ADA-compliant toilets?

Yes. ADA toilet installations are something Glen has real experience with — including taller comfort-height toilets, grab bar coordination, and other accessibility considerations.

What's a frost-free outdoor spigot and do I need one?

A frost-free spigot has the shut-off valve set back inside the warm part of your wall, so the exposed portion can drain and won't burst when winter hits. If you're in Western Mass with old-style spigots, they're worth upgrading.

My toilet runs constantly. Can it be rebuilt or do I need a new one?

Usually rebuilt. The flapper, fill valve, and flush mechanism are all replaceable parts. If the toilet body itself is sound, a rebuild costs far less than replacement.
