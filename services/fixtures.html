Frequently Asked Questions

When should I repair vs. replace my toilet? expand_more

If your toilet requires frequent part replacements, has a hairline crack in the porcelain, or lacks the flushing power of modern low-flow models, replacement is often more cost-effective.

My garbage disposal is humming but not turning. What should I do? expand_more

Turn off the unit immediately. Try using an Allen wrench in the bottom socket to manually rotate the impellers. If it still won't turn, it may be time for a professional replacement.

Can you install owner-supplied fixtures? expand_more

Yes, we frequently install high-end fixtures purchased by our clients. We ensure all parts are compatible with your existing plumbing and install them to the highest standards.
