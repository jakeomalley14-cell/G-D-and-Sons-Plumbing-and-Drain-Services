Signs You Need Heating Service

Don't wait for a total system failure. Look for these warning signs to prevent expensive repairs down the line.

device_thermostat

No Hot Water

If your showers are turning cold or the water never reaches temperature, your heating element or pilot may be failing.

water_drop

Leaking Tank

Any visible moisture around the base of your heater is a critical warning. Corrosion can lead to catastrophic tank failure.

volume_up

Strange Noises

Banging, popping, or whistling sounds indicate sediment buildup or pressure issues that require immediate attention.

Frequently Asked Heating Questions

How often should my boiler be serviced?

We recommend an annual safety inspection and tune-up every autumn to ensure peak performance and prevent winter breakdowns.

Is a tankless heater right for my home?

Tankless units are excellent for large families or homes with high hot water demand. We assess your peak usage to determine if the switch is cost-effective for you.

My radiator is cold at the top — what's wrong?

This is usually caused by trapped air. A simple "bleeding" process often fixes it, but if persistent, it may indicate a pump or zone valve issue.
