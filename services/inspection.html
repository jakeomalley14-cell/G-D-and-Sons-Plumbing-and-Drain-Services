Maintenance Intelligence FAQ

Is annual maintenance truly necessary for new homes?

Yes. Modern homes often have high-efficiency systems that require precise calibration to maintain their warranties. Early audits prevent small manufacturing defects from becoming major failures.

How does maintenance affect my energy bills?

Sediment buildup in water heaters and unoptimized boiler settings can force systems to work 20–30% harder. Regular flushing and tuning restore peak thermal efficiency.

What is included in Priority Support?

Priority Support means maintenance clients are dispatched first during high-volume periods — often within 2–4 hours of the call.
