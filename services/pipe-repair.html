Frequently Asked Questions

What is a slab leak and how do you find it?

A slab leak occurs when the pipes beneath your home's concrete foundation fail. We use non-destructive electronic amplification and electromagnetic pipe locators to find the exact source without breaking ground prematurely.

When is it better to repipe than repair?

If you have older galvanized steel pipes or have experienced more than two leaks in a single year, a whole-home repipe is often more cost-effective. It prevents future water damage and increases your property value.

How long does a typical pipe repair take?

Most minor leak repairs are completed within 2 to 4 hours. Larger jobs are scheduled and completed with the same precision and care.
