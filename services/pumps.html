Pump & Well FAQs

How often should I replace my sump pump?

Most high-quality sump pumps last between 7 to 10 years. However, if your pump runs frequently due to high groundwater levels, we recommend professional inspection every 3 years.

Why is my well pump "short cycling"?

Short cycling is usually caused by a waterlogged pressure tank. This puts immense strain on the pump motor and should be repaired immediately.

Do I really need a battery backup system?

Absolutely. Heavy storms that cause flooding often cause power outages. Without a battery backup, your sump pump is useless exactly when you need it most.
